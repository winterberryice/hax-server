Captcha Required

A captcha must be solved to create the room. Haxball uses reCAPTCHA v2.

  1. Open the captcha page by clicking this link.
  2. Solve the captcha there.
  3. After solving, you'll get a long text string (the token).
  4. Copy the token and paste it into the text area below.
  5. Click "Submit Token".
